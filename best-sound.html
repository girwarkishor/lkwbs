A BestSound Centre


C.C. Saha Ltd. has launched a BestSound Centre with Siemens Hearing Instruments in south Kolkata (Lake Road) in conjunction with Siemens Hearing Instruments. With a BestSound Centre in its fold, the company plans to provide world class customer care to Siemens hearing instrument users.

A BestSound Center represents an outlet that:

  • Exclusively promotes Siemens hearing care products
  • Provides professional services and best practices for hearing aid dispensing
  • Is end-consumer friendly
  • Ensures consistency, quality and trust 
  • Signifies state-of-art BestSound Technology* from Siemens.
    •  

    * BestSound Technology is an overarching umbrella term for combination of all technologies and features available in Siemens advanced digital hearing.
