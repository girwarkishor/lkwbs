With a rich legacy of over 80 years, C.C. Saha Ltd. is the oldest and leading hearing centre in eastern India. We dispense digital hearing aids of reputed global brands, namely Siemens, Starkey, Phonak, Bernafon and ReSound thereby offering a complete range of technologically advanced and state-of-the-art products that cater to all segments with assured quality and service.

Our four hearing clinics are located across Kolkata – Esplanade (central), Lake Road (south), Dunlop (north) and Barasat. We have qualified audiologists who do a thorough evaluation of one’s hearing capability and assess the degree of hearing loss. Based on the nature and extent of loss, we recommend the best type of hearing aid suited for the patient, who can then choose from our reputed brand of companies that have decades of global experience in manufacturing superior quality hearing aids.
