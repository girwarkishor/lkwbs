ABOUT US

Our four hearing clinics are located across Kolkata – Esplanade (central), Lake Road (south), Dunlop (north) and Barasat. We have qualified audiologists who do a thorough evaluation of one’s hearing capability and assess the degree of hearing loss. Based on the nature and extent of loss, we recommend the best type of hearing aid suited for the patient, who can then choose from our reputed brand of companies that have decades of global experience in manufacturing superior quality hearing aids.

OUR SERVICES

With a legacy of 80 years, we provide the entire spectrum of hearing solutions through our state-of-the-art testing facilities and our range of technologically advanced, superior quality digital hearing aids, including invisible and wireless products, catering to all segments and budgets.

EAR TESTING

Our team of qualified and highly motivated Audiologists & Speech Language Therapists provides all basic and advanced audiological tests including speech therapy and balance disorders.

HEARING AIDS

Siemens, Starkey, ReSound, Bernafon and Phonak are all renowned global brands that we dispense at our Clinics. These hearing aids are trendy, state-of-the-art and digitally advanced.

COUNSELLING

It is extremely essential that a patient is properly counseled and then provided with a suitable hearing aid. Our qualified audiologists are constantly undergoing training and updating themselves on the latest trends on digital hearing aids.

AFTER SALES SUPPORT

We are fully committed to providing the best possible service to our customers, who are free to walk into our clinic anytime for adjustments or reprogramming of their hearing aids. We also have a service and repair help desk for hearing aids.
