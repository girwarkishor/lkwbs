C.C. Saha Ltd. provides the entire spectrum of hearing solutions that begin with a thorough hearing evaluation by our Audiologists. We are one of the first companies over the years to import and use the very latest audiological instruments to establish the nature and extent of hearing loss. Our qualified audiologists inspect and test the patient's ability to hear tones across a range of frequencies. Based on this thorough evaluation, our technical team is able to recommend the best type of hearing aid based on hearing loss history and lifestyle preferences.

In keeping up with the technological advancements in the field of audiology, our Hearing Centres are fully furnished with anechoic chambers capable of handling hearing aid trials along with separate programming rooms for digital equipment. In addition, our RCI certified audiologists are trained with the latest and most sophisticated instruments to test the patient's hearing and accordingly provide the patient with the correct hearing aid based on requirement.

We have facilities for basic and advanced audiological tests which include:

  • Pure Tone Audiometry Tympanometry
  • Eustachian Tube Function Test Acoustic Reflex Study
  • Special Pure Tone based tests such as:
    1. SISI – Short Increment Sensitivity Index
    2. TDT – Tone Decay Test
    3. ABLB – Alternate Binaural Loudness Balancing
  • Speech Audiometry
  • Free-field Audiometry
  • Comprehensive Pediatric tests such as ASSR, ABR and OAE
  • VEMP
  • ECochG

    • Apart from these, we also provide a few other tests involving hearing and balance disorders such as:

  • Neurological ABR
  • Stacked ABR
  • ENG with Caloric Tests 
  • Glycerol Test
  • DPOAE

    • Besides these audiological facilities, we are a proud owner of a Laboratory which can be used to make customized molds for ITC (In the Canal) and CIC (Completely in the Canal) hearing aids. This is the first of its kind in eastern India making it possible to cater to the needs of outstation patients who need not wait for days to get a customized hearing aid.
